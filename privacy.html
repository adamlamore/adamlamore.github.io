WatchWords Privacy Policy

  • WatchWords allows users to create user-generated content, including audio recordings, for their private use within the app.
  • User-generated content is stored locally on the user's device within the app's private storage area. This content is retained indefinitely on the user's device. Users can delete this data at any time.
  • User-generated content is never shared or exported with any other application or transmitted or uploaded from the user's device.
  • WatchWords does not allow users to create an account or share their user-generated content.
  • User-generated content is automatically deleted and not retained when WatchWords is uninstalled.
  • Other than the specified user-generated content, WatchWords does not capture or collect any other data. This includes, but is not limited to: personal data, sensitive data, payment data, financial data, biometric data, usage data, user activity, derivative data, device data, geolocation data, messages, photos, or videos.
  • WatchWords does not have advertising and users will never receive any promotional or marketing communications.
  • WatchWords does connect to or access any external services.
  • WatchWords does not attempt to gather user data in any way from within the app, from the user's device, or any external source.
  • WatchWords cannot share/sell user data because WatchWords does not collect any such data.
  • WatchWords does not utilize artificial intelligence in any way.
  • WatchWords is available to users of all ages and complies with all required child privacy regulations.
